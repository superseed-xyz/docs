The [Arkantum Bridge](https://sepolia-bridge.arkantum.com/) facilitates the transfer of ETH and selected ERC-20 tokens between the Ethereum network and Arkantum.

To use the bridge for transactions:

1. Navigate to [Arkantum Bridge](https://sepolia-bridge.arkantum.com/).
2. Select **Connect wallet**.
3. Follow the prompts to connect your wallet.
4. Specify the amount of ETH or another supported asset you wish to move or redeem.

For answers to common questions regarding the Arkantum Bridge, consult the [Bridge FAQ](https://docs.arkantum.com/integrations/bridges/faq).
