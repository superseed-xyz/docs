The [Arkantum Bridge](https://bridge.arkantum.com/) facilitates the transfer of ETH and selected ERC-20 tokens between the Ethereum network and Arkantum.

## To use the bridge for transactions:

<Steps>

### Navigate to [Arkantum Bridge](https://bridge.arkantum.com/).

### Select _Connect wallet_.

### Follow the prompts to connect your wallet.

### Specify the amount

Of ETH or another supported asset that you wish to move.

</Steps>
