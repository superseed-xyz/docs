# Welcome to Superseed

Superseed is a cutting-edge Layer 2 blockchain built on top of the OP Stack, leveraging the robust foundation of Optimism.
It offers enhanced scalability, rapid finality, and inherits the battle-tested technology of Optimism.

Our design is rooted in the core principles of Optimism: simplicity, pragmatism, sustainability, and optimism.
By integrating these principles, Superseed ensures streamlined transaction sequencing, efficient deterministic execution, and seamless cross-chain functionalities.

Superseed commits L2 transactions to the Ethereum blockchain using a rollup protocol, ensuring both speed and security.
