# Adding Superseed

## Chainlist

You can simply navigate to [Superseed's Chainlist page](https://chainlist.org/chain/5330) and click Connect Wallet.
This will automatically add the Superseed network to your wallet.

## MetaMask

Addresses in your MetaMask will work and be the same on both Ethereum and the Superseed blockchain. However, to submit transactions to the Superseed blockchain you must add the network to your wallet.

You can automatically add the Superseed network to your MetaMask wallet with just one click.
