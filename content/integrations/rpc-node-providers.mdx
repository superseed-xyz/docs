# RPC Node Providers

## [Alchemy](https://www.alchemy.com/)

Alchemy provides fast and reliable private RPC endpoints alongside a full suite of tools to help support your development needs.

### Supported Networks

- Mainnet
- Sepolia Testnet

## [QuickNode](https://www.quicknode.com/)

QuickNode provides RPC endpoints for Superseed, along with websocket support and a suite of tools to help you ship.

### Supported Networks

- Mainnet
- Sepolia Testnet

## [dRPC NodeCloud](https://drpc.org/chainlist/superseed)

With dRPC NodeCloud, get access to Superseed's Mainnet and Sepolia Testnet, and another 184 blockchain networks, available for its free and paid user plans. 

### Supported Networks

- Mainnet
- Sepolia Testnet

## Superseed Public RPC

**Endpoints:**

- HTTP: https://mainnet.superseed.xyz
- WS: wss://mainnet.superseed.xyz
